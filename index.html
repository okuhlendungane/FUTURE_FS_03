About Us

Gambu-Line Hiring Services is a service delivery business owned by Mzolisi Ndungane, proudly operating in and around Mthatha. We specialize in providing essential event equipment to support a wide range of occasions, including weddings, funerals, church services, and family gatherings. Our focus is on reliable, on-time delivery and meeting the practical needs of our customers. Whether it’s keeping your refreshments and meat fresh with our mobile cold rooms, or ensuring your guests’ comfort with clean, high-quality mobile toilets, we help you host with confidence. At Gambu-Line Hiring Services, we bring convenience to your doorstep—so you can focus on your event without worrying about logistics or added strain on your home facilities.
